FAQs

Currently, only the Cyber Vault is available on this page. Users of the Cyber Vault receive cCYBER, which is transferable and allows for auto-compounded rewards. To learn more about the Vault and Stakepool, you can read the article.

You can initiate an unstake at any time, but there is typically a 7-day unstaking period. After 7 days, you can claim your CYBER.

By staking CYBER, holders gain the ability to submit and vote on governance proposals, directly influencing the ecosystem’s future. Additionally, CYBER stakers will receive a series of airdrops from Cyber’s ecosystem partners. As part of our community, you can also earn Link3 Score by staking within our platform.
